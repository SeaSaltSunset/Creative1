About

The Sword and Soul Project is a new effort started by a bunch of nerdy college kids
who enjoy looking at sharp pieces of metal to expensive for them to buy. We, well okay, I,
have been "playing" with swords ever since I was a kid and, although I may never use one in combat
I believe that swordsmanship is a hobby anybody can enjoy. To raise awareness, I set up this small
personality quiz website. Hopefully, it's gotten you and your friends a little more curious about the
world of swords. We don't have much here on the sight yet, but are continually expanding our collection.
Come back later to take the test again, and maybe by then we'll have added a new sword that matches you
even better than the last. See ya around, and
look sharp!
