You chose the Gladius!



Congratulations on your choice; you got the Gladius! The Gladius was the bread and butter for ancient
roman soldiers. It's short length makes it easy to hold in one hand, which is ideal for those who also carry shields.
Since it is so light, it can move deftly and quickly thru the air. Charge first with your shield, bash hard into your enemy,
and strike true to bring a swift end to your foe. As Yoda once said, "size doesn't matter".
